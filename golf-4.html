No additional costs for servers, licenses, backups, maintenance, updates ...

„GolfSuite as a Service“ is a so-called SaaS product (Software-as-a-service). That means you don’t need to invest in server infrastructure nor into IT maintenance. You can manage the software from any computer, tablet or smart phone. The cloud server infrastructure is provided, maintained by us and is included in the monthly fee.

Simple Fee Structure

No hidden costs, no extras for server infrastructure or software! Just a monthly fee that is composed of 200€ per property and 50€ per concurrent user.
