Federation Suite

The state of the art software consists of a B2B “software as a service” solution, the “GolfFederationSuite” and the “GolfSuite”, both purely web-based, fully integrated, with all modern functionalities for professional federation / club administration and management.

  • Customer and member administration
  • Management of direct or indirect memberships of federations
  • including a comprehensive billing module and CRM functionalities
  • Tournament organisation on federation and club level, including handicap-effective.
  • Handicap-administration on federation and club level.
  • Fully pursuant to the rules of the EGA handicap system in the versions of the national golf associations.
  • Control functions for automatic / manual transfer of tournament results to the hcp master data sheets.
  • API integration with the website of the federation (Official HCP list) and API for player related data
  • Data-Bridge between federations established at Nexxchange
  • Ordering system of federation ID cards
  • Tournament widgets for federation and club websites, players can register and pay the tournament registration fee online
  • Ready for World-Handicap-System
  • Multi-Language Support (available in more than 10 languages, English, Spanish, German, French, ...)

Our Offer

In order to offer small markets - with a limited budget - access to our cloud-based central federation database, as well es our comprehensive tournament module, we charge only €1,- per player and year, who is maintained in our database.
